## Course Resources

### Mapping Tools & Platforms

#### OpenStreetMap
- **Website:** [openstreetmap.org](https://www.openstreetmap.org/)
- **Description:** Free and open-source mapping platform where anyone can contribute geographic data
- **Tutorial:** [LearnOSM](https://learnosm.org/)

#### ArcGIS Online
- **Website:** [arcgis.com](https://www.arcgis.com/)
- **Description:** Cloud-based GIS mapping and analysis platform
- **NYU License:** Available through NYUAD Computing Services
- **Documentation:** [ArcGIS Online Help](https://doc.arcgis.com/en/arcgis-online/)

#### ESRI Storymaps
- **Website:** [storymaps.arcgis.com](https://storymaps.arcgis.com/)
- **Description:** Platform for creating interactive web-based narratives with maps
- **Gallery:** [Storymap Examples](https://storymaps.arcgis.com/stories)

#### QGIS
- **Website:** [qgis.org](https://www.qgis.org/)
- **Description:** Free and open-source Desktop GIS software
