Welcome to the fall 2022 edition of CDAD-UH 1033EQ: Data and Human Space!

This course explores the intersection of technology, geography, and human space. Over the next 14 weeks, we'll work together to understand how digital tools are reshaping our relationship with maps, place, and spatial data.

## What to Expect

- **Hands-on learning** with mapping tools and geospatial technologies
- **Project-based work** that combines analysis, storytelling, and critical reflection
- **Collaborative projects** that tap into real-world data and communities
- **Supportive community** where we learn from each other

## Getting Started

1. Review the [Course Overview](/overview/) page for complete course information
2. Check the [Schedule](/schedule/) to see what we'll be covering
3. Explore the [Resources](/resources/) page to familiarize yourself with the tools we'll use
4. Come to class ready to engage, ask questions, and explore!
