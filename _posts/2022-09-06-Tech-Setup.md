## Getting Ready: Technology Setup for Data and Human Space

Before our first lab session, please set up the following tools and accounts on your laptop. **No prior experience is required** — these instructions will walk you through it step-by-step.

### 1. OpenStreetMap Account

OpenStreetMap is free and open-source. To contribute and track your work, create an account:

- Go to [openstreetmap.org](https://www.openstreetmap.org/)
- Click "Sign Up" in the top right
- Follow the steps to create your account
- **Save your username and password** — you'll need them throughout the semester

**Time needed:** 5 minutes

### 2. ArcGIS Online

NYUAD has a license for ArcGIS Online. To access it:

- Contact NYUAD Computing Services to get your ArcGIS Online account
- You'll use your NYUAD login credentials
- Once activated, you can access [arcgis.com](https://www.arcgis.com/)

**Time needed:** 10 minutes (including contacting IT)

### 3. R and RStudio

We'll use R for data analysis. Both are free:

**Install R:**
- Go to [r-project.org](https://www.r-project.org/)
- Download R for your operating system (Windows, Mac, or Linux)
- Run the installer and follow the prompts

**Install RStudio (free version):**
- Go to [rstudio.com](https://www.rstudio.com/)
- Download RStudio Desktop (free version)
- Run the installer and follow the prompts

**Test your installation:**
- Open RStudio
- In the console (bottom left), type: `print("Hello, Data and Human Space!")`
- Press Enter — you should see the message printed

**Time needed:** 20-30 minutes

### 4. QGIS (Optional but Recommended)

QGIS is a free and open-source GIS software:

- Go to [qgis.org](https://www.qgis.org/)
- Download QGIS for your operating system
- Run the installer

**Time needed:** 15-20 minutes

### 5. Text Editor

You'll need a text editor for coding. Some good options:

- **VS Code** (recommended) — [code.visualstudio.com](https://code.visualstudio.com/)
- **Atom** — [atom.io](https://atom.io/)
- **Sublime Text** — [sublimetext.com](https://www.sublimetext.com/)

**Time needed:** 10 minutes

### 6. GitHub Account (Optional)

If you want to version control and share your code:

- Go to [github.com](https://github.com/)
- Click "Sign up"
- Create your account

**Time needed:** 5 minutes
